# Privacy Policy for RE-MOCT

**Last Updated:** June 25, 2026

Your privacy is a priority. Because RE-MOCT is designed as a local-first, CLI-based application, it operates under the following privacy principles:

### 1. Data Collection
RE-MOCT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 
* **No Telemetry:** The application does not track your usage patterns, keystrokes, or preferences.
* **No Network Dependency:** The application is designed to function entirely offline. It does not communicate with external servers, cloud services, or third-party APIs.

### 2. Media Handling
All audio files, playlists, and configuration files reside strictly on your local machine. RE-MOCT does not scan your file system for anything other than the directories you explicitly define for music playback.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Since RE-MOCT does not connect to the internet, it does not share data with, nor rely on, any third-party services.

### 4. Contact
If you have questions regarding this policy or the software's behavior, please reach out via the [GitHub repository issues page](https://github.com/RadMageIRL/re-moct/issues).
